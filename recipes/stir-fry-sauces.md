# Stir fry sauces

A few different ones.

### Basic sauce

- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 tbsp. soy sauce
- 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp. brown sugar
- 1/2 c. vegetable stock
- 1 1/2 tbsp. cornstarch

### Orange-ginger sauce

- 1" ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp. soy sauce
- 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
- 1/2 c. orange juice (or, the juice of one orange)
- 1 1/2 tbsp. cornstarch

### Black pepper sauce

- 3 tbsp. soy sauce
- 2 tsp. rice vinegar
- 1 tsp. brown sugar
- 1/2 c. vegetable stock
- 1 tsp. black pepper
- 1 tbsp. cornstarch

### Sweet & sour sauce

- 1 tbsp. soy sauce
- 2 tbsp. ketchup
- 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp. brown sugar
- 2/3 c. vegetable stock
- 1 1/2 tbsp. cornstarch
